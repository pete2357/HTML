Formula One


Formula One is a single seater open wheel motorsport. The cars are powered by a 1.6L V6 Hybrid engine. The power unit consists of 6 parts, they are Internal Combustion Engine, Motor Generator Unit - Kinetic, Motor Generator Unit - Heat, Energy Storage/Battery, Control Electronics and Turbocharger, every team is allowed to change these components which are in their component-pool, any additional component outside of pool, would lead to a grid penalty. There is one another component known as Gearbox, which can be changed after every 6 races, without any penalty

The lineup consists of 10 teams and 20 drivers, racing around 22 different circuits around the world, starting from Bahrain and ending at Abu Dhabi. Every team bring in new parts throughout this year-long fiesta to be a front runner some succeed but others don't, parts development is a tedious process.

Current champions are Max Verstappen(WDC*) and Mercedes AMG Petronas F1 Team(WCC).

Lewis Hamilton is the statistically greatest driver right now, with 103 Poles, 103 Wins, 183 Podiums, 7WDCs, 16 consecutive years of scoring podium and several others.
